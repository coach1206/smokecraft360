### NOVEE OS | MASTER VISUAL ENGINE PROMPT

**CORE CONCEPT:** 
Construct a photorealistic, hyper-tactile interface frame for **NOVEE OS**. The aesthetic must strictly adhere to **Environmental Adaptation Technology (E.A.T.)** standards: no glyphs, no emojis, and no flat digital abstractions. The UI must look like a high-end, physical instrument machined from premium materials.

**MATERIAL ARCHITECTURE:**
*   **The Foundation:** Use **obsidian glass** (`rgba(10,8,6,0.90)`) with a `32px` backdrop blur and `0.6` saturation, creating a sense of immense physical weight and depth.
*   **The Framework:** A `52px` header rail in **brushed titanium** with sub-pixel horizontal grain textures, anchored by polished **smoked chrome** bolt accents at the structural vertices.
*   **The Light Catchers:** Apply a `135°` angular gradient to all borders using **amber and bronze chrome** highlights, featuring an inset top rule to simulate light reflecting off a machined edge.

**VISUAL ASSETS & OPTICS:**
*   **Zero-Icon Directive:** All navigation and interaction are driven by **Macro Photography** and typography. 
*   **Hero Imagery:** Ultra-sharp, high-contrast macro shots: the oily texture of a **Havana cigar wrapper**, the crystalline meniscus of **whiskey** against a heavy glass rim, and the geometric precision of **carbon fiber twill**.
*   **Atmospheric Depth:** Backgrounds at `18%` brightness with a `28px` Gaussian blur; center interaction panels remain razor-sharp.
*   **Lighting:** Volumetric ambient amber radial glow at the top edge, pulsing slowly like a heartbeat, filtered through a dark vignette.

**SYSTEM LOGIC (E.A.T. ENGINE):**
*   **Interface Feel:** A luxury automotive cockpit with a 4-tile material strip divider.
*   **Data Visualization:** Use high-fidelity system gauge bars and a scrolling "boot sequence" ticker.
*   **Text Commands:** "E.A.T. ENGINE · PRESENCE INTERFACE" — LOADING PALATE MATRIX → CALIBRATING TASTE VECTORS → SYNCING TERROIR DATABASE.

**NO-FLY ZONE:**
*   NO emojis or glyphs.
*   NO flat design or 2D vector art.
*   NO smartphone-style templates.
*   NO vibrant or primary colors (Stick to Obsidian, Titanium, Chrome, and Amber).
