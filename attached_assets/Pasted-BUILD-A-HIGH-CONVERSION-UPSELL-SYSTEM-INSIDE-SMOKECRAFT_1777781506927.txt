BUILD A HIGH-CONVERSION UPSELL SYSTEM INSIDE SMOKECRAFT 360 / POURCRAFT 360 THAT FEELS NATURAL, PREMIUM, AND INTEGRATED INTO THE EXPERIENCE FLOW

---

CORE OBJECTIVE

Increase revenue by presenting upgrades and add-ons immediately after the user completes their experience.

Upsells must feel like:

* a better version of what they already want
* not a random sales pitch

---

UPSSELL TRIGGER POINT

Display upsells ONLY after:

👉 “Your Experience Is Ready”

This is the emotional peak of the journey

---

UPSSELL PANEL (RIGHT SIDE OR MODAL)

Create a dedicated section labeled:

“Upgrade Your Experience”

---

UPSSELL TYPES

1. PREMIUM UPGRADE

Show a better version of the selected cigar or drink

Example:

Standard:
Padron 1964

Upgrade:
Limited Reserve Maduro

Display:

* richer flavor description
* aged wrapper
* stronger profile

---

2. EXPERIENCE BUNDLE

Offer a full package:

* cigar + whiskey + pairing

Example:

“Full Lounge Experience – $95”

---

3. CUSTOMIZATION

Allow personalization:

* custom cigar band
* engraved box
* naming the experience

---

4. MEMBERSHIP (OPTIONAL)

Offer recurring value:

* exclusive cigars
* hidden pairings
* priority access

---

VISUAL DESIGN

Each upsell must:

* use real product images
* have gold border or glow
* be slightly elevated above base selection

---

INTERACTION

Each upsell card must:

* animate in (slide from right)
* respond on click (scale + glow)
* play click sound

---

PSYCHOLOGY TRIGGERS

Add:

* “Recommended for You”
* “Most Popular Upgrade”
* “Limited Availability”

Optional:

* stock counter (e.g., “Only 7 left”)

---

PRICING DISPLAY

Show:

* base option
* upgraded option
* price difference clearly

Example:

Standard – Included
Upgrade – +$25

---

CHECKOUT INTEGRATION

When user clicks:

* “Upgrade”
* or “Order Now”

Send selection to:

POST /create-checkout-session

Include:

* item name
* description
* price
* image

---

UX FLOW

User completes experience
→ sees result
→ sees upsell options
→ selects upgrade or base
→ proceeds to checkout

---

FINAL GOAL

User should feel:

“I want to upgrade this”

NOT:

“I’m being sold something”

---

FAIL CONDITION

If upsell feels:

* forced
* random
* disconnected

→ system fails
