Perform a FULL VISUAL + EXPERIENCE INTEGRITY AUDIT for the CraftHub / Axiom OS platform.

This is NOT a code audit.

This is a user-facing, visual, interaction, and experience audit.

Your job is to determine if the system FEELS real, consistent, and complete across all crafts.

---

## 🎯 AUDIT OBJECTIVES

Identify:

1. VISUAL QUALITY ISSUES
2. EXPERIENCE FLOW BREAKS
3. SUBCATEGORY MISMATCHES
4. DEAD OR NON-RESPONSIVE UI
5. INCONSISTENT DESIGN LANGUAGE
6. MISSING VISUAL FEEDBACK
7. LACK OF EMOTIONAL IMPACT

---

## 🧩 AREAS TO AUDIT (DO NOT SKIP)

---

### 🎨 1. VISUAL REALISM

Check ALL crafts:

SmokeCraft  
PourCraft  
BrewCraft  
VapeCraft  

Verify:

- Are real product visuals used (not placeholders)?
- Do objects have depth (shadows, lighting, layering)?
- Are images consistent in angle and lighting?
- Do overlays align correctly (no floating layers)?
- Do textures feel realistic (wood, glass, liquid, vapor)?

---

### 🧠 2. CRAFT IDENTITY DIFFERENTIATION

Each craft must feel like its own world.

Check:

SmokeCraft → warm, wood, gold, lounge  
PourCraft → glass, liquid, reflections  
BrewCraft → foam, texture, label energy  
VapeCraft → vapor, glow, modern  

Flag if:
- any craft looks like a copy of another
- same UI reused with only color change

---

### 🔘 3. INTERACTION RESPONSE

For EVERY button / selection:

Verify:

- Does something visually change immediately?
- Is there animation or feedback?
- Does the product update (not just text)?

Flag:
- clicks with no visible change
- delayed or confusing responses

---

### 🧩 4. SUBCATEGORY VALIDATION

Check all selection categories:

Example:
- wood types
- trims
- interiors
- liquids
- garnishes
- vapor types

Verify:

- Each option produces a DIFFERENT visual result
- No duplicate visuals under different names
- No missing asset mappings
- Naming matches visual output

---

### 🧭 5. EXPERIENCE FLOW

Each craft must follow:

1. Intro
2. Selection
3. Build
4. Refine
5. Reveal

Check:

- Is user guided step-by-step?
- Or dropped into a screen with no direction?
- Is there progression or just options?

---

### 🎬 6. REVEAL MOMENT QUALITY

Trigger reveal.

Verify:

- Does camera/scale change?
- Does lighting change?
- Does product feel “finished”?
- Is there sound or visual emphasis?

Flag:
- weak or non-existent reveal
- no emotional payoff

---

### 📱 7. TOUCHSCREEN USABILITY

Check:

- Are buttons large enough?
- Is spacing comfortable?
- Can this be used in a lounge setting?

Flag:
- small click targets
- cluttered UI
- hard-to-tap elements

---

### 🧪 8. VISUAL + DATA CONNECTION

Verify:

- Visual selections match backend config
- Saved builds match what user saw
- No mismatch between UI and stored data

---

### 🔄 9. CONSISTENCY ACROSS SCREENS

Check:

- fonts consistent?
- colors consistent?
- layout consistent?
- transitions consistent?

---

### 🚫 10. DEAD OR EMPTY STATES

Check:

- empty inventory
- missing visuals
- blank panels

Flag:
- any screen that feels unfinished

---

### ✅ VISUALLY STRONG AREAS

### 🧠 FINAL EXPERIENCE VERDICT

Is the system:
- Visually Demo-Ready
- Experience MVP-Ready
- Venue-Ready

Explain WHY.
