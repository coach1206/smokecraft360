Build a Level Requirement and XP Progression System for SmokeCraft 360.

GOAL:
Define clear, structured rules for how many items users must purchase and interact with to level up.

--------------------------------------------------
1. CORE METRICS (TRACK THESE)

For each user track:

- totalVerifiedOrders
- totalCigarsSmoked
- totalDrinksTried
- totalFullExperiences (cigar + drink + food)
- uniqueProductsTried
- XP

--------------------------------------------------
2. XP SYSTEM (VERIFIED ONLY)

ONLY award XP for verified orders.

XP rules:

- cigar purchase → +10 XP
- drink pairing → +8 XP
- food add-on → +5 XP
- full experience (cigar + drink + food) → +25 XP total
- trying new product → +5 bonus XP

--------------------------------------------------
3. LEVEL TIERS (HARD REQUIREMENTS)

Define exact thresholds:

LEVEL 1 — Explorer
- 0–5 verified orders
- XP: 0–100

LEVEL 2 — Enthusiast
- 5–15 verified orders
- at least 3 different cigars tried
- XP: 100–300

LEVEL 3 — Aficionado
- 15–30 verified orders
- at least 6 different cigars
- at least 3 drink pairings
- XP: 300–700

LEVEL 4 — Connoisseur
- 30–60 verified orders
- at least 10 unique cigars
- at least 5 full experiences
- XP: 700–1500

LEVEL 5 — Maestro del Fuego (TOP)

REQUIREMENTS:
- 60+ verified orders
- at least 15 unique cigars
- at least 10 full experiences
- at least 3 blends created
- XP: 1500+

--------------------------------------------------
4. PROGRESSION RULE (IMPORTANT)

User must meet BOTH:

- XP threshold
- activity requirements

If XP is high but requirements not met:
→ DO NOT level up

--------------------------------------------------
5. PROGRESS DISPLAY (UI)

Show:

- current level
- next level name
- XP progress bar
- checklist:

Example:

"To reach Connoisseur you need:"
✔ 30 orders
✔ 10 cigars tried
✔ 5 full experiences

--------------------------------------------------
6. COMBO INCENTIVE

Encourage behavior:

- show message:
"Full experience earns maximum XP"

--------------------------------------------------
7. LOCKED STATUS

Display:

"Level progression is based on verified lounge purchases only"

--------------------------------------------------
8. LEADERBOARD FILTERING

Rank users by:

- level first
- XP second

--------------------------------------------------
GOAL

Create a system where:

- users clearly understand how to level up
- progression requires real purchases
- variety and exploration are rewarded
- top status is difficult and respected
