ADD AN ANTICIPATION + SCORING + COMPARISON SYSTEM TO SMOKECRAFT 360 THAT BUILDS EXCITEMENT BEFORE THE FINAL REVEAL

This system must feel like a game where the user is building something valuable and wants to see their final result.

---

CORE OBJECTIVE

Do NOT show the final result immediately.

Create:

- buildup
- suspense
- delayed gratification
- reward at the end

---

1. PROGRESSION METER (ALWAYS VISIBLE)

Add a visible progress bar or meter:

- Shows % complete (ex: 40%, 60%, 80%)
- Updates after each step
- Use gold fill animation

Add text:

“Your blend is forming…”

---

2. LIVE SCORE BUILD (HIDDEN BUT TEASED)

As the user makes selections:

- Internally calculate a “blend score”
- Do NOT show full score yet

Instead show hints:

- “Strong profile developing”
- “Balanced pairing detected”
- “Premium blend in progress”

---

3. BUILD-UP TEXT (IMPORTANT)

After each step, display:

- “Good choice… this is shaping up nicely”
- “You’re creating something strong”
- “This one’s getting interesting…”

Keep it short, readable, and encouraging

---

4. LOCK THE FINAL RESULT (KEY FEATURE)

Before reveal:

- Show a locked panel or blurred card
- Label:

“Your Blend is Ready”

Add:

- blurred cigar + whiskey image behind it
- faint glow
- slight movement

---

5. REVEAL MOMENT (MAKE IT HIT)

User taps:

👉 “Reveal My Blend”

Trigger:

- background dims slightly
- sound cue (soft, premium tone)
- screen fades + zooms in
- card sharpens into focus

---

6. FINAL RESULT SCREEN

Display:

TITLE:
“Midnight Reserve”

SCORE:
Example: “92 / 100”

RATING:
- Smoothness
- Boldness
- Balance

VISUAL:
- Final cigar + whiskey pairing

---

7. COMPARISON SYSTEM (THIS IS WHAT MAKES IT ADDICTIVE)

After reveal, show:

“Compared to Others”

Examples:

- “Top 15% of blends created”
- “Stronger than average”
- “More balanced than most users”

Add a simple bar or meter showing position

---

8. PERSONALIZED LINE (IMPORTANT)

Add:

“Built for your taste: Smooth • Smoky • Relaxed”

This makes it feel personal

---

9. SHARE + REPLAY LOOP

Add buttons:

- “Try Again”
- “Compare Another”
- “Share My Blend”
- “Order This Experience”

Goal:

User wants to:
- improve their score
- try different combinations

---

10. VISUAL + MOTION RULES

- Reveal must be slower than other transitions
- Use fade + zoom (0.5–0.8s)
- Add glow pulse when score appears
- Product locks into final position

---

11. SOUND DESIGN

Add:

- soft “build-up” tone before reveal
- light “success” sound when score appears

Keep everything subtle and premium

---

12. FINAL EXPERIENCE FEEL

The user should feel:

- curious while building
- excited before reveal
- rewarded at the end
- motivated to try again

---

FINAL GOAL

Turn the system into:

- a challenge
- a game
- a repeatable experience

NOT:

- a one-time selection tool
- a static recommendation engine

---
