Build the SmokeCraft / PourCraft / BrewCraft pairing selection screen as a guided, step-based experience.

----------------------------------------
GLOBAL RULES
----------------------------------------
- No transparent cards
- No floating elements
- No random animations
- All visuals must be intentional and tied to the selection
- Cards must be visually descriptive (user should “see” the flavor)

----------------------------------------
STEP STRUCTURE
----------------------------------------

Top of screen:

"Step 1: Choose Your Flavor Profile"

Subtext:
"This determines your cigar and drink pairing"

----------------------------------------
FLAVOR CARDS (CRITICAL UPDATE)
----------------------------------------

Each flavor card must include:

1. Cream background (#F5EFE6)
2. CLEAR readable text
3. A REAL IMAGE representing the drink

----------------------------------------
BEER SYSTEM (MANDATORY FOR BREWCRAFT)
----------------------------------------

Each BrewCraft flavor MUST show real beer imagery:

Smooth & Mellow:
- Light golden beer
- Clean foam
- Lager or pilsner look

Spicy & Warm:
- Amber beer
- Reddish tones
- Stronger body

Smoky & Bold:
- Dark stout or porter
- Thick foam
- Deep brown/black tones

Rich & Sweet:
- Dark ale or stout
- Creamy texture
- Chocolate tones

----------------------------------------
CARD STRUCTURE
----------------------------------------

Each card:

Top:
- Image (takes up top portion of card)

Bottom:
- Title (Smooth & Mellow, etc.)
- Flavor notes

----------------------------------------
CARD INTERACTION
----------------------------------------

On click:

- Selected card:
  - scales up slightly
  - changes to gold highlight (#D4A85F)

- Other cards:
  - dim slightly

----------------------------------------
AI PANEL (RIGHT SIDE)
----------------------------------------

Must react to selection:

Example:

Smooth & Mellow:
"This lighter beer profile pairs well with mild cigars and smooth finishes."

----------------------------------------
NEXT STEP FLOW
----------------------------------------

After selection:

Show button:

"Continue to Pairing →"

----------------------------------------
MOVEMENT
----------------------------------------

Flow must be:

1. User sees step
2. User sees real drink visuals
3. User selects
4. System responds
5. User continues

----------------------------------------
VISUAL RULE (IMPORTANT)
----------------------------------------

User must be able to:

LOOK at the card
and instantly understand the flavor

WITHOUT reading

----------------------------------------
FINAL EXPERIENCE
----------------------------------------

This must feel like:

"A visual selection system driven by real drink identity"

NOT:
- generic cards
- placeholder UI
- text-only selection
