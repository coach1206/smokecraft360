Build a Brand / Distributor Monetization system into the recommendation engine and dashboard.

GOAL:
Allow venues and future brand partners to influence product visibility in a controlled, intelligent way.

FEATURES:

-----------------------------------
1. PRODUCT BOOST SYSTEM (VENUE CONTROL)
-----------------------------------

Update product model to include:

- boostLevel: number (0–3)
- sponsored: boolean

Logic:

- boostLevel adds hidden score:
   +1 per level

- sponsored products:
   +2 bonus

These should NOT override relevance, only enhance it.

-----------------------------------
2. DASHBOARD CONTROLS
-----------------------------------

In Inventory page:

Add:

- "Boost Level" selector (0–3)
- "Sponsored Placement" toggle

UI:
- simple slider or dropdown
- label: "Increase Visibility"

-----------------------------------
3. SMART PLACEMENT LOGIC
-----------------------------------

Modify recommendation scoring:

Final Score =
   baseScore
 + (boostLevel)
 + (sponsored ? 2 : 0)

BUT:

- Cap total influence so irrelevant products do NOT rank #1
- Maintain user trust

-----------------------------------
4. FEATURED PRODUCTS SECTION (UI)
-----------------------------------

In results page:

Add section:

"Featured Selections"

Display:
- sponsored / boosted products

Design:
- slightly different styling
- labeled "Featured"

-----------------------------------
5. ANALYTICS TRACKING
-----------------------------------

Track:

- how often boosted products are selected
- how often sponsored products convert

Display in dashboard:

- "Top Boosted Products"
- "Sponsored Performance"

-----------------------------------
6. FUTURE BRAND READY STRUCTURE
-----------------------------------

Prepare product model for:

- brandId
- campaignId

So later you can plug in:

- paid campaigns
- distributor deals

-----------------------------------
7. EXPERIENCE INTEGRITY
-----------------------------------

IMPORTANT RULE:

- Do NOT break recommendation logic
- Boosting should feel natural
- Always match user preferences first

-----------------------------------
GOAL:
Create a system where product visibility can be influenced, monetized, and tracked without breaking the user experience.
