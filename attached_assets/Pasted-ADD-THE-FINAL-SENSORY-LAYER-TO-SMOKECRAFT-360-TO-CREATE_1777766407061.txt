ADD THE FINAL SENSORY LAYER TO SMOKECRAFT 360 TO CREATE A FULLY IMMERSIVE, TOUCH-DRIVEN, LUXURY EXPERIENCE

This layer must make the system feel physical, responsive, and alive.

---

CORE OBJECTIVE

Every interaction must engage at least TWO senses:

- Visual (movement, lighting, product)
- Touch (response, motion)
- Sound (feedback, ambiance)

The system should feel like something you interact with physically—not just tap.

---

1. TOUCH FEEDBACK SYSTEM (CRITICAL)

All interactive elements must respond instantly:

- Buttons scale down on press (0.94)
- Cards slightly tilt or shift when touched
- Sidebar items move slightly when clicked (translateX + glow)

Add:
- micro “bounce back” effect after press
- subtle vibration-style motion (visual only)

---

2. CLICK SOUND SYSTEM

Add layered sound feedback:

- Soft “click” for navigation
- Slightly deeper “tap” for selections
- Subtle confirmation tone when completing a step

Rules:

- Volume low (premium feel)
- No harsh or loud sounds
- Instant playback on touch

---

3. AMBIENT SOUND (OPTIONAL BUT POWERFUL)

Loop a very low background audio:

- soft lounge chatter
- light jazz or instrumental
- glass / bar ambiance

Must be:
- extremely subtle
- not distracting
- auto-start or tap-to-start

---

4. PRODUCT SENSORY EFFECTS

Cigar + whiskey must feel alive:

- Cigar has subtle floating motion
- Add faint ember glow effect on tip
- Whiskey reflects light (slight shimmer)

Optional advanced:
- faint smoke drift from cigar tip (very low opacity)

---

5. LIGHT & REFLECTION SYSTEM

Add dynamic lighting effects:

- soft gold glow that shifts slightly
- light reflection sweep across cards (slow)
- subtle highlight on hover/touch

No aggressive animations—everything must feel smooth

---

6. SCREEN TRANSITION FEEL

Upgrade transitions:

- screens slide with weight (not fast)
- add slight blur during movement
- new screen “settles” into place

Goal:
Feels like panels physically moving, not digital switching

---

7. SWIPE PHYSICS (IMPORTANT)

Cards must behave like real objects:

- slight rotation when dragged
- resistance feeling (slower near center)
- snap into place when released

Visual only—no need for full physics engine

---

8. PROGRESSION REWARD FEEDBACK

After each step:

- show confirmation text:
  “Good choice… building your experience”
- brief glow or highlight pulse

At final reveal:

- subtle audio cue
- lighting shift
- product locks into final position

---

9. IDLE MODE (KIOSK FEATURE)

When no one is interacting:

- background slowly moves
- product gently floats
- subtle text appears:

  “Touch to begin your experience”

This draws people in without being aggressive

---

10. CONSISTENCY RULE

ALL sensory effects must be:

- subtle
- smooth
- consistent across all screens

No random or mismatched effects allowed

---

FINAL GOAL

The system must feel like:

- a premium lounge experience
- something tactile and responsive
- something people naturally want to touch

NOT:

- a website
- a static interface
- a noisy or flashy app
