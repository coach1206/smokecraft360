Build a Demand Engine and Proof System for SmokeCraft 360.

GOAL:
Automatically create, track, and display real customer demand so the platform generates proof for distributors and brands.

--------------------------------------------------
1. DEMAND CAPTURE SYSTEM
--------------------------------------------------

When a user:

- selects a product
- searches for something
- requests an item not in stock
- creates a blend

Capture:

- product name
- flavor profile
- category (cigar, alcohol, food)
- venueId
- timestamp

Store in:
demandEvents table

--------------------------------------------------
2. MISSING PRODUCT DETECTION
--------------------------------------------------

If a user selects or searches for a product NOT in venue inventory:

- mark as:
  "unfulfilled demand"

Store in:
missingDemand table

--------------------------------------------------
3. TREND ENGINE
--------------------------------------------------

Analyze demandEvents:

Generate:

- trending cigars
- trending flavors
- trending pairings
- trending blends

Update continuously.

--------------------------------------------------
4. DEMAND SCORE

For each product or flavor:

Calculate:
demandScore =

- selections
- requests
- orders
- blend usage

Rank highest demand items.

--------------------------------------------------
5. VENUE DEMAND DASHBOARD

Add section:

"Customer Demand"

Show:

- Most requested products
- Most requested but unavailable items
- Trending flavors
- Trending pairings

--------------------------------------------------
6. "YOU ARE MISSING SALES" ALERTS

If demand exists but no inventory:

Show:

"Customers are asking for this but you don’t have it"

List:
- product
- number of requests

--------------------------------------------------
7. PROOF PANEL (FOR YOU / SALES USE)

Create view:

"Demand Proof"

Display:

- top demanded cigars
- top demanded alcohol
- conversion rates
- real usage numbers

--------------------------------------------------
8. AUTO-GENERATED INSIGHT STATEMENTS

System generates lines like:

- "Customers prefer bold cigars 42% more"
- "Sweet flavor profiles are trending upward"
- "Bourbon pairings dominate 68% of sessions"

--------------------------------------------------
9. DEMAND → OPPORTUNITY ENGINE

Generate:

"Distributor Opportunities"

Example:

"High demand for Padron-style cigars across 2 venues — no supply available"

--------------------------------------------------
10. EXPORT / SHARE (SIMPLE)

Allow:

- copy insights
- basic export (JSON or text)

--------------------------------------------------
GOAL

Turn SmokeCraft 360 into a system that:

- captures real demand
- proves what customers want
- exposes missed revenue
- creates leverage for distributor deals
