---

🚨 CORE OBJECTIVE

Integrate PourCraft 360 (wine/cocktail experience) as a configuration-based theme that runs on the SAME system as SmokeCraft 360.

This must be a multi-tenant, multi-theme platform.

---

🚨 NON-NEGOTIABLE RULES

- DO NOT create a standalone PourCraft app
- DO NOT duplicate backend logic
- DO NOT hardcode theme behavior
