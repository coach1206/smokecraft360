REBUILD SMOKECRAFT 360 FOR MEN AGES 35–75 WITH AGING VISION WHILE MAINTAINING A LUXURY CIGAR LOUNGE EXPERIENCE

This system must be visually rich but extremely easy to read, touch, and understand.

---

CORE RULES

* Everything must be readable at a glance from arm’s length
* Large text, high contrast, no strain
* Buttons must feel solid and easy to tap
* No clutter, no small UI elements
* Every action must feel rewarding and responsive

---

TYPOGRAPHY (CRITICAL)

* Headings: 32px–40px (serif, bold, gold or dark brown)
* Section titles: 20px–24px
* Body text: minimum 18px (never smaller)
* Increase line spacing for readability

DO NOT use thin or light fonts

---

CONTRAST FIX (VERY IMPORTANT)

* Replace pure black backgrounds with deep brown (#1A1410)
* Use a warm cream card (#F5EBDD) for ALL main content
* Dark text on light card for readability
* Gold only for accents and highlights

Text must NEVER sit directly on busy backgrounds

---

LAYOUT STRUCTURE

LEFT SIDEBAR:

* Keep steps, but increase spacing
* Each step must be clearly readable
* Active step = strong gold highlight
* Completed = checkmark + dim gold

CENTER:

* Large cigar + whiskey visual
* Keep it bold and clear

RIGHT:

* Light premium card (main reading area)

---

BUTTON SYSTEM (TOUCH-FIRST)

Buttons must feel like physical objects:

* Minimum height: 60px
* Large padding
* Rounded edges
* Strong visual separation

PRIMARY BUTTON:

* Gold gradient
* Slight glow
* Label example: “ORDER NOW”

SECONDARY BUTTON:

* Soft glass background
* Clear border

GHOST BUTTON:

* Simple text, lower emphasis

Every button must:

* slightly scale down when pressed
* show instant feedback

---

INTERACTION SIMPLIFICATION

* Limit choices per step (no overwhelming options)
* Use clear labels instead of abstract names
* Show immediate feedback after each selection

Example:

User selects flavor:
→ Display:
“Smoky selected — bold and rich”

---

VISUAL STIMULATION (IMPORTANT)

Add subtle motion to keep attention:

* Slow floating cigar animation
* Gentle light movement on card
* Background slightly shifts (very slow)

DO NOT use fast or flashy animations

---

REVEAL SCREEN (MAKE IT SATISFYING)

* Fade in slowly
* Center the cigar + whiskey pairing
* Use large readable title:

“Midnight Reserve”

* Add simple line:

“Built for your taste: Smooth • Smoky • Relaxed”

---

REMOVE STRAIN

* No small text
* No tight spacing
* No overlapping visuals
* No low-contrast gold-on-dark text

---

FINAL EXPERIENCE GOAL

The interface should feel:

* easy to read
* comfortable to use
* visually rich but not overwhelming
* like a premium experience without effort

The user should never have to squint, guess, or search
